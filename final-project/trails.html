Trail Difficulty Rating System

Easy

Generally flat with minimal elevation gain. Suitable for beginners and families with children.

Moderate

Some elevation gain and potentially uneven terrain. Suitable for hikers with some experience.

Difficult

Significant elevation gain, challenging terrain, and/or longer distances. For experienced hikers.
