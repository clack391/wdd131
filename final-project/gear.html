Essential Hiking Gear

Proper equipment can make the difference between an enjoyable hike and a miserable (or dangerous) experience. Browse our recommended gear for mountain hiking.

Essential Gear

Hiking Backpack

A good hiking backpack should have comfortable straps, adequate capacity for your needs, and convenient pockets for organization.

Recommended for: All hikes

Water Bottle/Hydration System

Always carry enough water for your hike. Hydration bladders make drinking convenient while on the move.

Recommended for: All hikes

Map and Compass

Even with GPS technology, a physical map and compass are essential backup navigation tools.

Recommended for: All hikes, especially remote locations

Footwear

Hiking Boots

Sturdy boots with ankle support and good traction are essential for rugged terrain and longer hikes.

Recommended for: Moderate to difficult trails

Trail Running Shoes

Lighter than boots but still offering good traction, these are suitable for well-maintained trails.

Recommended for: Easy to moderate trails

Hiking Socks

Proper hiking socks provide cushioning, moisture management, and blister prevention.

Recommended for: All hikes

Clothing

Layered Clothing

A base layer for moisture wicking, mid-layer for insulation, and outer layer for weather protection.

Recommended for: All hikes, especially in changing weather

Sun Hat/Beanie

Sun protection in warm weather and heat retention in cold weather are essential for comfort.

Recommended for: All hikes

Gloves

Lightweight gloves for cold mornings or heavier ones for cold-weather hiking.

Recommended for: Cold-weather or high-altitude hikes

Safety Equipment

First Aid Kit

A compact kit with bandages, antiseptic wipes, pain relievers, and other essentials for trail emergencies.

Recommended for: All hikes

Emergency Whistle

A simple but effective way to signal for help if you're injured or lost.

Recommended for: All hikes

Headlamp/Flashlight

Essential if your hike runs longer than expected or for early morning/late evening hiking.

Recommended for: All hikes, especially longer ones

Extra Comfort Items

Trekking Poles

Provide stability on uneven terrain and reduce impact on knees during descents.

Recommended for: Moderate to difficult trails, especially with elevation changes

Sunglasses

Protect your eyes from UV rays, especially at higher elevations where sun intensity increases.

Recommended for: All hikes, especially in sunny conditions

Camera

Capture your hiking memories with a dedicated camera or a smartphone with a good camera.

Recommended for: All hikes, especially in scenic areas
